Name

  • Bitcamp is stylized as “bitcamp” in the context of the logo, but should always be written as “Bitcamp” in text.
  • Bitcamp is monolithic. Avoid writing “Bitcamp hackathon” or amending it otherwise unless absolutely necessary.
  • Different Bitcamp events are identified by year. This is not a movie franchise.
  • When noting Bitcamp’s date, write Bitcamp [year]. Specific dates should be written in this structure: April 10-12, 2015.
